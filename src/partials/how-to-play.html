How to play

  • Merge items

    Combine identical items to create more powerful versions.

  • Built your castle

    Use merged resources to upgrade and expand your kingdom

  • Recruit heroes

    Merge items to unlock and level up powerful allies.

  • Defend and conquer

    Strategically deploy your heroes to win battles and unlock new lands.

  • Complete quests

    Solve puzzles, fulfill tasks, and gather treasures to progress your kingdom.
