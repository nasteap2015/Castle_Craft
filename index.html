About the game

Castle Craft: Merge Quest is a unique blend of strategy, adventure, and merging mechanics. As a ruler of a crumbling kingdom, your mission is to rebuild your castle, gather a powerful army, and defend your land from looming threats. Unlock treasures, discover new territories, and write your legacy in this epic merge quest.
